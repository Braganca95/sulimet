Metalworking Electrical Systems

Who We Are

The Sulimet Group is composed of four companies operating in two countries, Portugal and Morocco.

We specialize in wiring harnesses and metal parts, serving markets including automotive, food and the construction industry.

Mission

To provide innovative and high-quality industrial solutions, contributing to the success of our partners and clients.

Vision

To be a reference in the industrial solutions market, recognized for excellence, innovation and commitment to sustainability.

Values

Quality, integrity, innovation, customer commitment and social and environmental responsibility.

Our History

1989

The company was founded as Suleve, later renamed Sulimet, beginning production of wiring systems for the automotive industry in Santa Maria da Feira, Canedo, Portugal.

2008

Sulimet was founded, establishing a unit for the production of metal and plastic parts still in Portugal.

2009

Achieved ISO 9001 certification in Portugal and started operations of the metalworking unit in Tangier, Morocco.

2010

Wiring systems production for the automotive industry began in Tangier, Morocco.

2011

Achieved IATF 16949 certification in Tangier, Morocco.

2015

Achieved IATF 16949 certification in Portugal.

2017

Achieved H&S, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 certifications in Morocco.

2018

Achieved ISO 9001 certification in Tangier, Morocco.

2019

Implemented a new ERP system and created the sales department.

2021

Division of Sulimet, Lda and creation of Sulimet Wiring, Lda.
